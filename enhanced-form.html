Common Cybersecurity Best Practices

  • Use strong, unique passwords for each account.
  • Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible.
  • Keep software and operating systems updated.
  • Be cautious of phishing emails and suspicious links.

Steps to Respond to a Cyber Incident

  1. Identify and isolate the affected systems.
  2. Assess the extent of the damage.
  3. Notify relevant authorities and stakeholders.
  4. Recover data and strengthen security measures.
